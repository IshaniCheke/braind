# Braind — AI Campaign Generation Studio

Braind is an AI-powered campaign generation studio that turns a campaign brief, uploaded references, and visual direction into a structured marketing campaign kit.

The most successful branding campaigns share a few common traits: a clear core message, an emotional hook, and widespread memorability. Braind was built around that idea — helping users move from a rough campaign concept to a more complete, organized, and reviewable brand campaign.

## What Braind Does

Braind helps users generate campaign assets from a single brief. A user can enter campaign details, upload brand references or inspiration files, choose the output formats they need, and generate a complete campaign kit.

The generated campaign kit includes:

- Campaign strategy
- Core message, emotional hook, and memorability device
- Instagram, LinkedIn, billboard, poster, website hero, and email assets
- Evaluation scores for clarity, grounding, memorability, emotional strength, brand alignment, and format fit
- Editable AI-generated revisions
- AI-generated campaign visuals
- Image preview and download support
- Supabase-backed campaign storage and generated asset storage

## Why We Built It

Marketing and branding work often starts with scattered inputs: a short idea, a mood board, a product description, a few brand references, and a vague target audience. Turning that into a structured campaign usually takes multiple steps across strategy, copywriting, visual planning, and review.

Braind brings those steps into one workflow.

It is designed to help creators, founders, and small teams quickly move from a rough campaign brief to a structured, multi-format campaign kit that can be reviewed, edited, and improved.

## Features

### Campaign Brief Input

Users can enter key campaign details such as:

- Brand name
- Product or service
- Campaign objective
- Target audience
- Tone
- Campaign theme
- Geographic region
- Keywords
- Additional creative instructions

### Multi-Format Asset Generation

Braind can generate campaign content for multiple formats, including:

- Instagram post
- LinkedIn post
- Billboard
- Poster
- Website hero
- Email campaign

### Reference Uploads

Users can upload supporting materials such as:

- Brand guideline PDFs
- Product brochures
- Mood boards
- Screenshots
- Previous campaign references
- Text files

Uploaded references are processed and used as context during campaign generation.

### Campaign Evaluation

Each generated campaign asset is evaluated across multiple dimensions:

- Brand alignment
- Grounding
- Compliance and safety
- Message clarity
- Emotional strength
- Memorability
- Format suitability

This helps make the output more reviewable instead of simply generating content without feedback.

### Revisions

Users can revise generated text assets directly inside the platform by providing edit instructions.
